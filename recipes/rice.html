rice

description

fluffy rice seasoned with garlic and butter.

ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of rice
  • 1/4 cup chopped yellow onion
  • 1.5 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2.5 cups of chicken broth
  • 4 tbsp butter

directions

  1. melt butter
  2. sautee onion & garlic
  3. add broth, bring to boil
  4. add rice, simmer
  5. allow rice to rest for 5 minutes
  6. finish with butter, fork fluff and garnish
